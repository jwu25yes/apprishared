Why AppriShared

Good for you. Good for farmers. Good for your community.

Every Saturday, perfectly good local produce gets hauled back or tossed simply because the market day ended. That's a waste of food, money, and the farmer's effort. AppriShared closes that gap — turning surplus into something the whole community benefits from.

🥬
Fresh produce at half the price
A $15 box holds produce with $35–45 retail value, still at peak freshness from that morning's harvest.
🌾
Real revenue for local farmers
Vendors earn money they'd otherwise write off. Every box sold helps a small SF farm stay viable.
♻️
Zero food wasted
Nothing gets tossed. Every vegetable in your box was rescued from the end of a real market day.
🏘️
Hyper-local, always
We source only from your local farmer's markets. Your food travels miles, not thousands of miles.
